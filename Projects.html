CURRENTLY WORKING ON: xG Simulator

My latest project is an xG simulator for soccer in C++

This new stat in soccer was introduced a few years back and it basically tries to predict the probability of a regular player scoring a shot

However, this is often based on historical data from what I have understood, without taking into account the physics and how the angles work

I wanted to predict how are these shots calculated and if my hypothesis is correct and how close am I to those models

At some point I will implement a way to make that map available like where is a player shooting from and what did it result on
